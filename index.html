Welcome!

I am a Research Staff Scientist in the Center of Computational Network Intelligence, Information and Systems Sciences Lab (ISSL) at HRL Laboratories, LLC. I received my PhD from the Department of Computer Science at the University of California, Santa Barbara in 2012. I was a member of the Vision Research Lab and the Center for Bio-Image Informatics, adviced by Professor B.S. Manjunath. My research interests are Graph Mining, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Big Data Analytics.
